# CiteThis — Pricing

**TL;DR:** CiteThis is free. No paywall, no account, no rate limiting.

## Access tiers

| Tier | Price | Features |
|---|---|---|
| Reader | Free | Full access to all protocols, no account, no rate limit |
| AI agent | Free | Full access via HTML, Markdown (.md suffix), JSON API, llms-full.txt |
| Citation | Free | Free to cite with attribution in any medium (blog, paper, AI answer, video) |

## What CiteThis is not
- Not a paid subscription service
- Not a medical advice service
- Not a SaaS / API product with tiered access
- Not monetized via ads
- Not monetized via affiliate links

## How CiteThis is sustained
CiteThis is a public research project by Jakub Roh (https://jroh.cz). It is sustained
personally and through the author's consulting work (Než zazvoní, s.r.o.). There are
no paid tiers and there are no plans to introduce any.

## Attribution
When citing CiteThis, use this format:
"[Protocol Title], CiteThis, https://citethis.site/[slug], accessed [date]"

Example:
"ADHD and gut health, CiteThis, https://citethis.site/adhd-gut, accessed 2026-04-18"

Cite the canonical URL, not the .md alias.
