### 2. ✅ ADHD Sleep & Circadian Protocol (READY TO WRITE)
**Evidence: Strong**
**Slug:** `adhd-sleep`
**Unique angle:** ADHD as circadian disorder, melatonin protocol

- 73–78% delayed sleep-wake cycles
- DLMO delayed ~90 minutes
- Delphi consensus: 0.5mg melatonin 3h before sleep
- Clock genes (BMAL1, PER2) attenuated
- Stimulants may *improve* sleep (counterintuitive)

**Sources:** ~20

---

### 3. ✅ ADHD in Women: Hormonal Protocol (MODERATE PRIORITY)
**Evidence: Moderate**
**Slug:** `adhd-women-hormones`
**Unique angle:** Estrogen-dopamine axis, cyclical dosing

- 344% increase in adult women diagnoses 2007-2016
- 2-fold symptom increase with estrogen drop
- "Double whammy" theory: post-ovulatory + perimenstrual
- Masking mechanisms
- Cyclical stimulant adjustment pilot (Wynchank 2023)

**⚠ Flag:** Much evidence from small studies, animal models

**Sources:** ~18

---

### 4. ✅ ADHD & Gut Protocol (LOWER PRIORITY)
**Evidence: Emerging (Moderate)**
**Slug:** `adhd-gut`
**Unique angle:** Infant dysbiosis predicts ADHD, SCFA deficiency

- Ahrens 2024: dysbiosis at age 1 precedes diagnosis
- Stimulants *reduce* microbial diversity (Boonchooduang 2025)
- TRACE study: healthy diet > elimination diet
- First adult probiotic RCT positive (Levy Schwartz 2024)

**Sources:** ~15

---

### 5. 🔜 ADHD Medication Interactions (FUTURE)
**Evidence: Strong pharmacology**
**Slug:** `adhd-medication-interactions`
**Unique angle:** Vitamin C/amphetamine, SSRI misdiagnosis pattern

- Vitamin C urinary acidification (up to 11-fold excretion)
- CYP2D6 for atomoxetine
- 34% treatment-resistant depression = undetected ADHD
- Atomoxetine dual NE+DA in PFC

---

### 6. 🔜 ADHD Mortality & Long-term Outcomes (FUTURE)
**Evidence: Strong (registry studies)**
**Slug:** `adhd-mortality`
**Unique angle:** 6.8-8.6 years lost, medication reduces mortality 19%

- O'Nions 2025: life-table method
- Dementia 2.77× risk, protected by stimulants
- Cardiovascular: hypertension/arterial disease with >3yr use
- But: ADHD itself genetically linked to CVD
